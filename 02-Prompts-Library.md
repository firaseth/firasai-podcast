## Script Writing Prompts

### Full Episode Script (30 minutes)
```
Write a 30-minute podcast script for FirasAi on [TOPIC].

Structure:
- HOOK (30 seconds): A surprising stat, story, or question
- INTRO (2 min): Welcome, today's topic, why it matters
- SEGMENT 1 (8 min): The main problem
- SEGMENT 2 (8 min): The counterintuitive solution
- SEGMENT 3 (8 min): Real-world examples
- OUTRO (3 min): Recap, call to action, tease next episode

Tone: Bold, data-driven, slightly rebellious, accessible
Include natural pauses marked as [PAUSE], laughs as [LAUGH], and
emphasis as *word*.
Avoid filler words. Make it sound human, not robotic.
```

### Solo Episode Script
```
Write a 15-minute solo FirasAi podcast script where I'm sharing
[PERSONAL STORY / FRAMEWORK / LESSON].

Requirements:
- Open with a moment of tension or vulnerability
- Use the "hero's journey" structure
- Include 1 specific story with sensory details
- Provide 3 actionable takeaways
- Close with a memorable one-liner
- Total word count: ~2,250 words
```

### Interview Question Generator
```
I have a guest coming on FirasAi who is [TITLE, EXPERTISE, ACCOMPLISHMENT].

Generate 15 interview questions organized into:
- 3 warm-up questions (about their origin story)
- 5 core questions (about their expertise)
- 4 challenging questions (contrarian or deeper)
- 3 rapid-fire questions (fun, personal)
- 1 closing question (with a call to action)

Make questions open-ended. No yes/no answers.
```

### Cold Open Generator
```
Write 5 cold open options (under 30 seconds each) for a FirasAi episode
about [TOPIC].

Options should include:
- A shocking stat
- A weird/surreal scenario
- A provocative question
- A mini-story with a cliffhanger
- A bold contrarian statement

Each should make the listener NEED to keep listening.
```

## Guest Outreach Prompts

### Cold Outreach Email
```
Write a cold outreach email to invite [NAME] on FirasAi (podcast at the
intersection of AI, business, finance, crypto, and sports; audience:
entrepreneurs and investors; downloads: [NUMBER]).

Email requirements:
- Subject line (3 options)
- Personalized opening referencing their recent work on [SPECIFIC THING]
- Compliment their expertise with specifics
- 1-2 sentence pitch on why their story matters to my audience
- Clear ask: a 30-45 min interview
- Promise: what they'll get (exposure, content, etc.)
- Soft deadline

Keep it under 200 words. Sound human, not salesy.

before recording.

- Episode topic and 3-5 questions I'll ask (not all)
- What to wear (if video)
- How to prepare
